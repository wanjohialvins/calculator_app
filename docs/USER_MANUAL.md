## 1. Introduction
Eragon Calc is a sleek, web-based calculator designed for a modern operating system experience. It provides high precision, real-time feedback, and a persistent history log for your calculations.

---

## 2. Interface Overview
- **Display**: Shows your current input and the last operation result.
- **Keypad**: Standard calculator layout with additional scientific features like $\pm$ and $\%$.
- **History Panel**: Located on the right (desktop) or below (mobile), showing your calculation trail.

---

## 3. Basic Operations
- **Addition (+)**, **Subtraction (-)**, **Multiplication (√ó)**, **Division (√∑)**: Standard arithmetic.
- **Clear (C)**: Resets the current display.
- **Percentage (%)**: Converts the current value to a decimal.
- **Sign Toggle (¬±)**: Flips the current value between positive and negative.

---

## 4. History Management
Every calculation you perform is automatically added to the **History Log**.
- **Visual Trail**: Review past results at a glance.
- **Persistent Storage**: Your history stays with you as long as you use the same browser.
- **Clear History**: Use the "Clear" button in the history header to wipe your log.

---

## 5. Keyboard Shortcuts
For power users, Eragon Calc supports full keyboard integration:
- **Numbers**: `0-9`
- **Operators**: `+`, `-`, `*`, `/`
- **Calculate**: `Enter` or `=`
- **Clear**: `Esc` or `Delete`
- **Decimal**: `.`

---

## 6. Exporting Data
You can export your calculation history to a **CSV file** for use in Excel or other spreadsheet software:
1. Navigate to the History panel.
2. Click the **Export CSV** button.
3. Save the generated file to your device.

---

## 7. Troubleshooting
- **Display shows "Error"**: This usually happens when you try to divide by zero or perform an invalid sequence. Press **C** to reset.
- **History not showing**: Ensure your browser supports `localStorage` and that you are not in Private/Incognito mode.
